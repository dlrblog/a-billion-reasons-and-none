Author's note

This is where you speak plainly — not through metaphor, but as yourself.

Write what the poem couldn't quite hold. The reason you started. What you were afraid to say directly.

It doesn't have to be long. A few true sentences matter more than paragraphs.

On writing this

You might write about when this came to you — a specific moment, a particular light, something she said that stuck.

Or why poetry and not a letter. Or both.
